# The Restaurant Kitchen Analogy

- Agents are like assistant chefs in a kitchen.
- Skills are like recipe cards that define how to accomplish a specific task.
- The ingredients are the Context - chicken, onion, salt, butter, etc.
    - Each chef/agent only gets the ingredients (context) they need to perform their cooking task.
- The Tools are the stove, frying pan, knife, spatula, etc., that are needed to complete the task.
- Instruction files are like rules for the kitchen.
    - Wash hands frequently
    - Call out loudly: "Behind!", "Hot!", "Sharp!", "Corner!"
    - Label and date everything

As an engineer, I’m the master chef, making sure everything is going smoothly.
